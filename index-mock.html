CIMA makes it easier than ever for you to inform your city about issues with public infrastructure. In less than a minute you can report about any issue that needs addressed.

Getting started with CIMA

Once you download the app, creating a report is simple.

Step 1

Provide a location

The app will automatically record your location when you report at the location of the issue.

Step 2

Snap a Photo

Providing a photo will assist us in determining you the exact issue and saves you time.

Step 3

Answer some quick questions

Answer just two or three quick multiple-choice questions to help us narrow down the issue quickly

Step 4
