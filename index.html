Voice your concerns.

CIMA makes it easier than ever for you to inform your city about issues with public infrastructure. In less than a minute you can report about any issue that needs addressed.

On-The-Go Reporting

Creating a report is fast and simple. When you see an issue, it only takes a moment to report it using these 4 steps:

Step 1

Provide a Location

The reporting software will automatically record your location when you start a new report at the scene of the issue.

Step 2

Snap a Photo

Providing a photo will assist us in determining the exact issue and saves you time.

Step 3

Answer Some Quick Questions

Answer just two or three multiple-choice questions to help us categorize and file your report.

Step 4

Write a Description (optional)

A description helps to narrow down issues that are hard to dertermine from a photo alone.
